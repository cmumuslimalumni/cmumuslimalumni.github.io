What We Do

Mission

A united community of Muslim alumni committed to uplifting one another and empowering future generations of Muslim students at CMU.

Vision

To build a thriving network of Muslim alumni from Carnegie Mellon University that fosters lifelong support, spiritual + professional growth, and community.
